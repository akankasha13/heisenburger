Paneer Bhurji

A delicious and easy to make recipe that's loved by everyone


Paneer Bhurji is a delicious and quick North Indian dish made from crumbled paneer (Indian cottage cheese), saut√©ed onions, tomatoes, and a flavorful blend of spices. It's a versatile recipe that can be enjoyed as a wholesome breakfast, lunch, or dinner option. Packed with protein and bursting with flavors, Paneer Bhurji is a favorite among vegetarians and is incredibly easy to prepare.Enjoy making this recipe and sharing it with your loved ones!


Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Some spices
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Coriander
  • Cumin seeds

Instructions

  1. Crumble the Paneer into small chunks.
  2. Toss chopped onion ginger and garlic in a heated pan.
  3. Add the spices and prepare a thick paste.
  4. Add the crumbled paneer.
  5. Cover it with a lid and cook for 5mins.
  6. Garnish it with some coriander and serve hot.Enjoy!
