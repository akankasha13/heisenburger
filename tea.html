Chai

Description

Chai, also known as Indian tea, is more than just a beverage; it's a comforting ritual cherished across India and around the world. Made by brewing tea leaves with milk, water, sugar, and aromatic spices like ginger, cardamom, and cloves, chai is a flavorful and invigorating drink that brings warmth to your soul. Its rich aroma and soothing taste make chai the perfect drink for any occasion. Paired with snacks like biscuits, pakoras, or samosas, it creates an unforgettable experience that unites people with its simplicity and charm.

Ingredients

  • milk
  • Tea leaves
  • Ginger
  • sugar
  • water
  • cardamom

Instructions

  1. Add everything
  2. Boil it
  3. Pour in a cup
  4. Serve hot
